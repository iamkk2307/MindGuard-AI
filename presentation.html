06

Methodology & AI Models

1. Text Preprocessing

Raw text is noisy. We apply:

  • Lowercase conversion
  • Removal of punctuation & special characters
  • Stopword removal (removing "and", "the", etc.)
  • Lemmatization (converting "running" to "run")

2. Feature Extraction (TF-IDF)

Converts text to numerical vectors based on word importance rarity.

3. Classification Model (XGBoost)

A Gradient Boosting algorithm that builds an ensemble of decision trees.

  • Why? Handles imbalance well, provides feature importance for XAI(Explainable Artificial Intelligence), and is faster than neural networks.

Challenges & Solutions

üö© Challenge 1

Imbalanced Data: Real-world data has far more "Normal" cases than "Crisis" cases.

Solution: Used synthetic data generation and balanced sampling techniques to ensure fair training.

üö© Challenge 2

Misclassification: Model flagged "happy" text as "Anomaly" due to unseen vocabulary.

Solution: Augmented training set with diverse positive affirmation dataset.

üö© Challenge 3

Resource Constraints: Running ML on standard laptops (8GB RAM).

Solution: Optimized TF-IDF max features (8000) and used lightweight XGBoost configuration.
